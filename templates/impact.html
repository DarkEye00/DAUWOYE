DAUWOYE . WHO WE ARE

We are always championing for various causes within the country and in Kisii.

Health Care
As Daraja United Women And Youth Empowerment Organization,
we have a health care center setup to cater for the health needs of
citizens based in Kisii. To learn more about the services,
kindly visit our Health Care Center in Kisii Town Near Mwembe.

HIV Awareness
HIV is a menace in the country and around the world and as such,
We pride ourselves in raising awareness in HIV abstinence and
prevention in order to curb its spread within the country.

Gender Based Violence
DAUWOYE engages community leadership to educate them that
there are better ways of solving conflicts other than violence.
We also campaign for laws and policies that protect and
promote women's rights.

Awareness on FGM
FGM is a violation of girl's and women's human rights.
It has adverse effects on women including risks during
childbirth. We are always on the forefront of combating
FGM together with other organizations within the country.

Education
We help needy young girls and boys within the society
get access to education with all the required equipment.
Partnering with other organizations and well wishers,
we bring a smile to every child in need of education.
