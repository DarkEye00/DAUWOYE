DAUWOYE . OUR IMPACT

We are always championing for various causes within the country and in Kisii.

Health Care Program
As an organization, we have one health facility with her annex, which
offer medical services where we provide treatment of primary illness.
We treat out patients but our facilities are currently being prepared for
inpatients as well. These are for income generation and sustainability.
To learn more about the services, kindly visit our Health Care Center
in Kisii Town both in Mwembe and Daraja Mbili.
Our health facility was recognized by TIKO project supported by
Triggerise as the best health facility in kisiicounty.Taking small steps
every day with quest of making our society better where human rights
are respected and promoted

HIV Awareness
HIV is a menace in the country and around the world and as such,
We pride ourselves in raising awareness in HIV prevention in order
to curb its spread within the country.

Gender Based Violence
DAUWOYE engages community leadership to educate them that
there are better ways of solving conflicts other than violence.
We also campaign for laws and policies that protect and
promote women's rights.

Advocacy Program
We collaborate and network with other organizations like
the Human Rights Organizations and Reproductive Health
Network to amplify the voices of women and youths.
Through capacity building, we always champion for laws
and policies that accommodate women's views and are
co-opted on decision-making. We educate the youths and girls
to know and make use of their rights. We do mobilization at the
grassroots through communiy outreaches to raise awareness.

Education
Through our partners, we were able to give 200 girls
uniforms, shoes, and school bags to ensure they go
back to school. Every child deserves an opportunity to
go to school. We help vulnerable young girls and boys within
the society get access to education with all the required equipment.

Socio-economic
We identify vulnerable youths, mostly teenage mothers who
access training and mentorship on entrepreneurship. After that,
we offer them seed grants through our partners to attain self-
reliance. DAUWOYE does a follow up on the women they
empower to ensure that they do not lag and improve their lives
and that of their families
