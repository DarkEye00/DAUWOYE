DAUWOYE . OUR IMPACT

We are always championing for various causes within the country and in Kisii.

Health Care Program
As an organization, we have one health facility with her annex, which
offer medical services where we provide treatment of basic illness.
We have several wards in our clinics where we admit our patients.
We treat both in patients and out patients, and offer treatment to both
kids and adults.These are for income generation and sustainability.
To learn more about the services, kindly visit our Health Care Center in
Kisii Town Near Mwembe.

HIV Awareness
HIV is a menace in the country and around the world and as such,
We pride ourselves in raising awareness in HIV abstinence and
prevention in order to curb its spread within the country.

Gender Based Violence
DAUWOYE engages community leadership to educate them that
there are better ways of solving conflicts other than violence.
We also campaign for laws and policies that protect and
promote women's rights.

Advocacy Program
We collaborate and network with other organizatios like
the Human Rights Organizations to amplify the voices of
women and youths. Through capacity building, we always
champion for laws and policies that accommodate women's
views and are co-opted on decision-making. We educate the
youths and girls to know and make use of their rights. We do
mobilisation at the grassroots through communiy outreaches
to raise awareness. We collaborate and network with other
organizatios like the Human Rights Organizations to amplify
the voices of women and youths.

Education
Through our partners, we were able to give 200 girls
uniforms, shoes, and school bags to ensure they go
back to school. Every child deserves an opportunity to
go to school. We help needy young girls and boys within
the society get access to education with all the required equipment.

Socio-economic
DAUWOYE does a follow up on the women they empower to ensure that
they do not lag and improve their lives and that of their families.
We empower women and youths by offering seed grants through our partners.
We identify vulnerable youths, mostly teenage mothers who access training and
mentorship on entrepreneurship.We identify vulnerable youths
(mostly teenage mothers) and women who access training and mentorship on
entrepreneurship. Later on, they are given seed capital to attain self reliance.
