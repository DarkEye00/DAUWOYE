Welcome To DAUWOYE

Daraja United Women and Youth Empowerment is a Kenyan, women/youth â€“ led national NGO focused on women and youth empowerment. We work in the rural areas of Kenya, but are currently in Kisii and Nyamira counties. It was incepted in 1997 as a self-help group by the name of Bidii Women Group. The focus of the organization was to create economic empowerment opportunities for women. The organization took part in several program focusing on HIV/AIDs, sexual reproductive health in general. In December 2007, we registered as an NGO (Non-Governmental Organization) under our current title, Daraja United Women and Youth Empowerment (DAUWOYE).
