YOUTH ENGAGEMENT

The steps below are used to keep our youths engaged in within their society. Our measures are not limited to the steps below and apply various methodologies in our day-to-day interactions.

  • We educate the youth on the advantages of abstinence and use of protection during sex to curb spread of STIs,STDs and HIV/AIDS. During such engagements ,they are also advised on family planning so as to bring up a family within their living capabilities
  • The youths are also trained and mentored on how to become leaders.
  • They are also given opportunities by the organization to actively participate in decision-making processes.
